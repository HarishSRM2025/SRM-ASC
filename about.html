Our Roots

College History

Founded in 1985 by the visionary educationist Dr. T. Rajaram, SRM Arts & Science College was established with the singular mission of democratising quality higher education and making it accessible to students from all walks of life across the region.

What began as a modest institution with three departments and fewer than 200 students has blossomed into a comprehensive multi-disciplinary college recognised for its academic standards, student achievements, and community outreach programmes.

Over the decades, the college has achieved numerous milestones — securing NAAC accreditation, introducing postgraduate and research programmes, and building industry partnerships that have placed graduates in leadership positions globally. The founders' philosophy of education as a tool for social transformation continues to guide every decision the institution makes.

Today, we stand at the forefront of educational innovation, embracing technology and global perspectives while remaining deeply rooted in our founding values of integrity, inclusivity, and intellectual rigor.

Journey

College Milestones

1985
Foundation & Inception
SRM Arts & Science College was established by Dr. T. Rajaram with a founding batch of 180 students across three departments — Arts, Science, and Commerce — and a charter to transform regional education.
2002
First NAAC Accreditation
The college received its first National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) accreditation with a B+ Grade, marking formal recognition of its academic standards and institutional quality.
2009
Postgraduate Expansion
Eight new postgraduate programs were launched across science, humanities, and commerce disciplines, doubling student enrollment and establishing the college's research culture.
2017
NAAC Grade A & Campus Expansion
A landmark year — the college was re-accredited with NAAC Grade A and inaugurated a new academic complex, sports arena, and digital library, elevating campus infrastructure to international standards.
2020
Digital Transformation Initiative
Launched a comprehensive digitisation of academic operations — including a Learning Management System, smart classrooms, and online examination infrastructure — ensuring continuity through the global pandemic.
2022
NAAC A+ — Highest Accreditation
The college achieved NAAC A+ accreditation with a score of 3.62 out of 4.00 — the highest tier of recognition — cementing its position as a premier institution of higher learning in the state.
2024
Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship
Inaugurated a dedicated Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship in partnership with leading industry players, offering students incubation support, mentorship, and seed funding opportunities.

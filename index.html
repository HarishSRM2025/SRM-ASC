About Our College

SRM Trichy Arts & Science College is a premier NAAC accredited institution committed to providing quality education and holistic development. Located in Mannachanallur, Tiruchirappalli, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to meet the evolving needs of industry and society.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on research and innovation, we nurture students to become competent professionals and responsible citizens.
